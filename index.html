About Me

Hi, I’m Saif Ullah Khalid—a passionate nanotechnology researcher, dedicated educator, and relentless innovator in the field of materials science. My work focuses on harnessing the power of ZnO nanoparticles to create antibacterial fabrics with real-world impact. With an M.Phil in Solid State Physics from the University of the Punjab, Lahore, I specialize in merging cutting-edge science with practical applications, especially in functional textiles that are both protective and sustainable.

Join me as I explore the possibilities of nanotechnology to reshape how we think about safety, sustainability, and innovation!

Research Highlights

ZnO-Coated Fabric

  • Enhanced Antibacterial Power: Unique coating for cotton fabrics with exceptional antibacterial properties.
  • Superhydrophobicity: 157° contact angle for water repellency without compromising breathability.
  • Sustainable Solutions: Green synthesis minimizes environmental impact.

Advanced Antibacterial Testing

  • Extensive testing using well-diffusion and disk-diffusion methods.
  • Analysis of nanoparticle treatment variations on antibacterial efficacy.
